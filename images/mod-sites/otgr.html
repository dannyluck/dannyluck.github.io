Off the Grid - Russia

Discover 'Off the Grid - Russia' a Russian map, famous for its vast wilderness, abundant snow, challenging roads, and stunning Siberian scenery. Explore untouched natural landscapes, encounter diverse wildlife, and embark on thrilling expeditions through its challenging terrain, promising unforgettable adventures in Siberia's wild beauty.

the map is compatible with every other Russian map in ETS2. As of version 1.2 for game version 1.49 it features the cities of Talnakh, Norilsk, Dudinka and Potapovo. If you use it in a map combo, make sure you place it somewhere at the top below the background map.
